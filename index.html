Chrono Escape

Story

In the year 3025, a scientist named Alex discovers an ancient relic that opens a mysterious time portal. As he steps closer, a horde of cybernetic monsters, attracted by the portal’s power, ambush him. With no time to think, Alex leaps through the portal to escape. When he opens his eyes, he finds himself in an ancient era, surrounded by gigantic, menacing dinosaurs and primitive wilderness.

The world he has entered is full of danger, but it also holds the key to his survival — a way back to his time. However, before he can return to the future, Alex must navigate this prehistoric world, battling fierce creatures, building a stronghold, and scavenging for ancient resources to survive. To escape, he must locate hidden relics of time, each piece essential to opening the portal back to the year 3025.

Gameplay Features

  • Fighting Dinosaurs: Face different species, each with unique behavior and attacks. You’ll need to strategize to defeat these ancient creatures.
  • Base Building: Gather materials and build a fortified base to protect yourself from the elements and predators.
  • Resource Scavenging: Collect resources from the past, like ancient stones, plants, and metals to craft tools and weapons.
  • Time Relics: Search for hidden time relics scattered throughout the world. Each relic holds part of the secret to reopening the time portal and returning home.
  • Dynamic Weather & Day/Night Cycle: The environment is harsh, with changing weather and dangerous creatures that appear at night.
  • Survival Mechanics: You must manage hunger, health, and stamina, as well as defend your base from both dinosaurs and hostile human tribes who inhabit the past.

Objective

Your primary mission is to survive and find the necessary time relics to return to 3025. Along the way, you'll uncover secrets of the time portal, the monsters that pursued you, and the fate of the ancient civilizations that once existed before your arrival.
